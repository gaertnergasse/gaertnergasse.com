Julia Hohenwarter – All White

Julia Hohenwarter – All White

13th October – 3rd November 2017

For her solo exhibition Julia Hohenwarter investigates the movements that occur, and conditions at work when staging an exhibition. She applies thoughts about shells and armors protective of interiors into our space.

invited by Handbag (Julija Zaharijević and Julia Znoj)
