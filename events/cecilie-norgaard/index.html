<< Gärtnergasse

Cecilie Norgaard

4th – 30th April 2016

invited by Handbag

chairs and cupboards loomed together so that

though each was separate they seemed inextricably involved.

the looking glass whitened its pool upon the wall. the real flower

on the window-sill was attended by a phantom flower. yet the phantom

was part of the flower, for when a bud broke free the

paler flower in the glass opened a bud too.
